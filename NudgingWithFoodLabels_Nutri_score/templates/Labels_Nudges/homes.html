Personalized Recipes Recommendations

Welcome :)

Welcome to our study! The aim of this study is to understand how we can use technology to suggest new recipes to people, based on their perferences. This study is part of the PhD research of Ayoub El Majjodi at the University of Bergen, Norway, under supervision of Prof. Dr. Christoph Trattner and Dr. Alain Starke.
Please carefully read the procedure followed in this study to give your informed consent for voluntary participation*.

Procedure

You will be first asked to answer a few questions about your personal characteristics and to indicate your preferences for a specific food category by clicking and rating 10 different recipes.
Based on your personal preferences, we will then present you a list of recipe recommendations. You will be asked to select the recipe that you like the most and would like to cook at home. Afterwards, you will be asked a few questions about the chosen recipe and the decision-making process.

Duration and compensation

The study takes around 5-7 minutes to complete. You will be compensated with $ XX. By clicking the 'Start' button, you agree to have read and understood this consent form.
